ABOUT

The Exhibition, 18/06/2017

A story of art

Welcome to The Exhibition. Come explore our innovative curation of the most exciting artistic movements, situated in the iconic grounds of Emmanuel College. Immerse yourself in creative history through the finest music, aesthetics, and entertainment that May Week has to offer. Be inspired by our story of art: refreshments will be provided.

WORK

Emmanuel College May Ball is now recruiting

We are looking for motivated and enthusiastic individuals to join our vibrant workforce and help make this year’s May Ball unforgettable.
For all roles, diligence and the desire to work as part of a team is required. Training and orientation sessions will also be provided to ensure that you are comfortable in your chosen role.

More information can be found on the application form below.

Apply to Work

Roles Avaliable

General Work

Emmanuel May Ball operates a half on/half off shift system for general workers. By working either the first half or second half of the ball, general workers are welcome to enjoy the other half absolutely free, as payment for their shift.

Supervisor

Paid work for impressive applicants looking for leadership and managerial roles. Court Supervisor will be responsible for a variety of workers on the night within their designated section. We will also require specialised supervisors, covering Entertainment and Drinks.

Clear-up

A paid role for clearing up the morning after the Ball.
